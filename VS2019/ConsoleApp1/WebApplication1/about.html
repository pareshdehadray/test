CO2 less Cloud App Features

CO2 Less CLOUD is designed considering the below factors:

Feature Moto

As the world’s poplation continues to grow, our challenge should not be how to grow more food, but to feed more people while wasting less of what we already produce

Application Focus

Our Application is focused to minimize 3 out of top 4 causes of CO2 emission (Electricity , Transport and Agriculture)

Sustainability

Our application is created considering Healthy Environment, Healthy Economy, Healthy Society

Awareness

Our application creates awareness among individuals by generating graphs/reports highlighting food wastage DB cafeteria vendor can easily predict the amount food to be prepared which will reduce the wastage.
